Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Thank you for using AndroidMD — a simple and lightweight Markdown editor for Android.

1. Overview

AndroidMD is designed with your privacy in mind. The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • No account or login is required
  • No personal information (name, email, phone, etc.)
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No advertising identifiers or third-party analytics
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers beyond what is strictly necessary for basic app functionality

3. Permissions

The app only requests the minimum permissions necessary to function as a Markdown editor:

  • Storage / File access — to read and save your Markdown files

No unnecessary permissions are requested.

4. Data Storage

All your Markdown files are stored locally on your device only. Nothing is uploaded to any server.

5. No Ads, No Payments, No Tracking

AndroidMD contains no advertisements, no in-app purchases, and no tracking mechanisms.

6. Children's Privacy

The app does not target children and does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted here with an updated "Last updated" date.
